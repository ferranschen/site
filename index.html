About Me

Hello, my name is Wei-Hao Chen. I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University and a member of the Human-Centered Software Systems Lab under the guidance of Professor Tianyi Zhang. My research primarily focuses on Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Software Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence. I am currently developing tools to help data scientists using several techniques such as LLM, Artificial Intelligence, and Programming-by-Demonstration. Prior to my Ph.D. studies at Purdue, I worked as a software engineer at Realtek Semiconductor Corp in Taiwan. My previous research was centered around Cryptography.
